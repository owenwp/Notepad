Jigsawed 2010 Ephemeron Industries Contracted Development

iTunes Link

An existing application which I was contracted to enhance. My most significant contributions have been a major refactor and rewrite of the puzzle view itself primarily to support zooming and panning, iPad support, and in-app purchase.

In addition to adding the ability to zoom in and out of the puzzle view at high resolution, I also re-designed the puzzle pieces so that they could be joined together and manipulated seamlessly as groups, without any shadow overlap artifacts. A great deal of work went into optimizing this code due to memory limitations on older devices.

The addition of iPad support was fairly straightforward. My model for its design was the Mail application, using a master-detail split view to present the main menu and image picker. In-app purchase was implemented using JSON to retrieve product information from a server. Once a purchase is made, the pack of puzzle images is retrieved as a Zip file. The user experience is designed to be familiar to users of the App Store, with preview images and progress bars for downloads.
