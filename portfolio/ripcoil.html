Ripcoil 2016 Sanzaru Games

A competitive online VR game where players attempt to throw discs past each other in futuristic arenas. Designed for standing play, you lean to the left and right to drive a hovering platform across the front of your goal.

Implemented player inverse kinematics system and body size compensation. Created systems for standing mode tracking and calibration, and worked on refinements for the leaning-based movement system. I lead the effort for optimization and scalability, implementing options and fallbacks for low end hardware. I was also responsible for fixing general image quality and graphical issues, and refining shader effects and animation blueprints.
