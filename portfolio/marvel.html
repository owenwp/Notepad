Marvel Powers United VR 2016-2018 Sanzaru Games

A cooperative online VR game where players embody classic Marvel super heroes and fight against waves of enemies and villains.

Implemented player inverse kinematics system. Developed resolution independent 3D UI with refinements to work with dynamic temporal supersampling. Created character material pipeline and shaders for previewing different lighting models in Substance Painter. Advised on rendering, inverse kinematics, GPU optimization, scalability, lighting, effects, lending assistance where needed toward the end of production.
